  About Us

AGEM is a company dedicated to the vision of a world where every child has access to meaningful education, focused on understanding, which is supported by constant measurement of the success of delivery, and the growth of understanding of the child. This in turn is supported through the use of technology, sophisticated analysis and feedback.

Over the last decade AGEM has been researching the best mechanisms for delivering on this vision through the development of low power-drain technologies working with children in difficult circumstances in various parts of India through providing partial implementations of the system now developed, and validating its methods and processes. Today AGEM stands ready to implement the full scale of its system in hundreds of schools.

The key characteristics of the AGEM system are:
- Scalability
- Measurability
- Affordability
- Effectiveness
