  Innovations In Education  

Child Led Social Equity Audit is a report by an external consultant on a project conducted over a period of three years in  the tsunami affected villages of Tamil Nadu by Shonu Chandra and Samar Singh for Plan in India. The title of the project was Pictures for Life.

PFL2 is a report for the Pictures for Life project by Shonu Chandra and Samar Singh during the second year of the project and introduces the idea of a Performance Alert to highlight areas where performance needs to be looked at. This is derived from the data collected by the children during the Social Equity Audit survey.

Distractor Analysis is a report by Shonu Chandra and Samar Singh on measuring the efficacy of learning outcomes  from a film created by children. It introduces the idea of real time feedback devices as well as the concept of using simple distracters to determine where elements of the delivered content were of less interest to the child audience.
